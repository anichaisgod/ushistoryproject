The Laws of Neo Mund Colony

  • 1.Every citizen of the colony has a right to practice the religion of their choice without prejudice or public judgment

  • 2.Every significant decision, will be put to a vote before it is ratified

  • 3.Leaders are voted upon by the government

  • 4.Colonists have the right to defend and support themselves in the best manner that they can, while still conforming

  • 5.All citizens will have the right of owning their own property, and those who are born within the colony are given land for free when they come of age

Where Neo Mund Colony is Located

Settling in the southern region of the new world because the warmer climate will result in better farming, and an easier way of life. This climate also makes the soil very fertile, and the livestock and crops that can be sold will be a great economic benefit to the colony, and those who live in it.

Neo Mund Colony Constitution

Thy honorable people of Neo Mund, agree that in the best interest of future colonists, pledge ourselves to being free of prejudice towards different cultures and religion, in order to make our society safer than others. People have the right of self defense, that is reserved for when absolutely necessary. We establish this constitution, while we remain loyal to our Great Britain, but we oppose the laws and we make our own. Together we stand united in our new world, where we believe in secundo casue.
